Satay Recipe

Summary

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Additional Time: 1 hrs 5 mins
  • Total Time: 2 hrs 30 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic crushed
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 2-inch piece of fresh lemon grass (white part only)
  • 2 pound beef top sirloin, trimmed
  • 4 12 inc-long metal skewers

Directions

  1. Whisk together brown sugar, fish sauce, garlic, onion, oil, soy sauce, coriander, ginger, cumin, turmeric, and cayenne pepper in a mixing bowl until smooth.
  2. Bruise lemongrass by hitting it lightly several times with the back of a large chef's knife; mince lemongrass and add to marinade.
  3. Cut breef sirloin into strips about 2 1/2-inches long and 1/8 inch-thick. Sitr beed into marinade until beed is completely coated, about 1 minute. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for 2 to 4 hours.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat. Lightly oil the grate.
  5. Remove beef from marinade and shake ogg excess marinade. Thread 1/4 of the meat onto each metal skewer. Discard remaining marinade.
  6. Arrange skewers on the preheated grill; cook until meat stops sticking to the grill, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip skewers and continue cooking until meat is well browned and shows grill marks, 2 to 2 1/2 mites. Flip skewers once more; cook until meat is still slightly pink, about 2 minutes. Transfer skewers to a platter; let rest for 2 minutes before serving.
